# Maai — demo descriptions (ZOE hackathon)

Paste-ready inputs for the live demo. Both describe the same woman:
the atypical female cardiac presentation (BHF: symptoms that
"don't look like the poster").

## English (demo beat 1)

For the past three weeks I've been absolutely wiped out by the middle of
the day, even when I've slept. I get out of breath just going up the
stairs, which never used to happen. There's a dull ache in my jaw and
across my upper back that comes and goes, and some days I feel sick for
no reason. My husband says I'm just run down, but it doesn't feel like
that.

## Chinese (demo beat 2 — the contrast moment)

最近三个星期，我到中午就觉得特别累，就算睡够了也一样。上楼梯会喘不上气，
以前从来没有这样过。下巴和上背部有一种隐隐的酸痛，时有时无，有些天还会
无缘无故觉得恶心。家里人说我只是太累了，但我觉得不是那么回事。

## Why these

- Same woman, same story, two languages — the demo contrast in one move
- Time window stated ("three weeks") — patterns, not an emergency
- The dismissal is IN the text ("my husband says I'm just run down") —
  the record captures being dismissed, which is the whole thesis
- Atypical cluster per BHF: fatigue, breathlessness, jaw/back ache, nausea
- No chest-pain-right-now language — never demo an active emergency
